TrustOrigin

Use and Interpretation

TrustOrigin provides neutral records of declaration and time. It does not provide advice, judgement, or assurance. Reliance on any record is a decision made independently by the relying party.

No Duty of Care

TrustOrigin does not assume a duty of care to any party through the provision of records, documentation, or system outputs.

The system does not monitor, review, verify, or evaluate the substance of declared artefacts.

Interpretation Responsibility

Interpretation of any TrustOrigin record is context-dependent. TrustOrigin does not determine how a record should be interpreted or applied.

Any reliance on a TrustOrigin record is undertaken at the sole discretion and risk of the relying party.

Third-Party Use

TrustOrigin records may be referenced or presented to third parties. TrustOrigin does not control, endorse, or assume responsibility for how records are used or represented by others.

TrustOrigin is a neutral recording infrastructure.
