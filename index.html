Declarative Governance Infrastructure for AI & Digital Continuity

TrustOrigin provides a neutral recording layer for structured institutional declarations relating to AI system usage, digital artefact lineage, vendor dependency exposure, and operational continuity posture.

Why Declarative Governance Matters

Modern organisations operate within layered digital infrastructures. AI systems influence outputs. External vendors introduce structural dependency. Artefacts evolve across versions. Migration exposure accumulates silently.

TrustOrigin records structured declarations addressing these exposures at defined points in time. It does not replace operational systems. It records statements about them.

Operational Boundary

TrustOrigin does not verify artefacts, assess regulatory compliance, determine legality, certify systems, or resolve disputes. It records structured declarations and preserves their existence.

TrustOrigin is a neutral declarative governance infrastructure.
