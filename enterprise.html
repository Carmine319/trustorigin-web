Enterprise Governance Licensing

TrustOrigin Enterprise Licensing provides deterministic continuity infrastructure for organisations operating within governance-critical environments. The licence framework is structured for institutions requiring structural documentation integrity, audit resilience and export survivability across multi-year operational horizons.

Enterprise engagements are designed for construction firms, developers, infrastructure programmes, research institutions, AI-driven enterprises and risk-sensitive organisations where documentation ambiguity presents measurable operational exposure.

TrustOrigin does not provide legal adjudication, regulatory certification or contractual interpretation. The Enterprise Licence provides structured continuity architecture only.

What the Enterprise Licence Covers

The Enterprise Licence grants structured deployment of the TrustOrigin deterministic continuity backbone across one or more vertical environments, including Construction, AI Governance, Ethics, Research and Insurance-aligned risk infrastructure.

Licensing covers architectural continuity infrastructure rather than workflow software. It preserves documentation posture, lineage clarity and migration survivability without interfering in operational autonomy.
