Neutral Escrow Ledger

The Neutral Escrow Ledger enables chronological custody recording and declarative escrow of digital artefacts within the TrustOrigin registry.

It records the existence, timing, and declared status of escrowed materials without exercising control, release authority, or adjudicative function.

This vertical is suitable for two-party or multi-party contexts where neutral custody presence must be recorded without enforcement.

This system does not certify outcomes, enforce conditions, adjudicate disputes, or provide legal judgement.
