TrustOrigin

Register an Artefact

This page records the intent to declare a digital artefact within the TrustOrigin system.

Registration does not imply validation, certification, endorsement, legality, or correctness.

What Registration Does Not Do

  • Judge accuracy, truth, safety, or legality
  • Certify or endorse content
  • Confirm ownership or authorship
  • Resolve disputes

Current Status

Public self-registration interfaces are not yet enabled. This page documents the registration model and governance boundaries.

TrustOrigin is a neutral recording infrastructure.
