30-Day Institutional Pilot

Pilot Objective

To implement TrustOrigin within a controlled institutional environment and assess structured declaration recording, vendor dependency articulation, and continuity posture documentation.

Scope

  • Up to 50 declarative registry entries
  • Vendor dependency mapping template
  • Migration readiness posture documentation
  • Deterministic export simulation
  • Continuity dossier draft

Duration

30 days from activation.

Deliverables

  • Pilot Trust Receipts
  • Institutional Continuity Dossier (draft)
  • Governance Gap Summary
  • Migration Risk Commentary

Pilot Fee

£5,000 – £8,500 depending on institutional scope.
