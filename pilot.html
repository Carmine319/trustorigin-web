Institutional Pilot Access

TrustOrigin offers a limited, evaluation-only institutional pilot providing supervised access to its neutral registry infrastructure.

The pilot is designed to enable assessment of suitability, governance alignment, and operational fit prior to any licensing discussion.

Pilot Conditions

Pilot access is time-limited to a maximum of thirty (30) days and operates in a human-gated, operator-mediated mode.

Automated execution, cloud-based processing, APIs, and continuous operation are not enabled during the pilot phase.

Participation in the pilot requires a neutral public attribution or citation acknowledging evaluation or use of the TrustOrigin infrastructure.

Pilot access does not constitute a licence, entitlement, or contractual commitment. Continued use beyond the pilot period requires execution of a licensing agreement.
