Synthetic Media & Declarations

This vertical enables neutral, timestamped recording of declarations relating to AI-assisted, synthetic, or composite digital artefacts.

It records statements of process, intent, or disclosure without analysing, classifying, or interpreting the artefacts themselves.

This system does not detect, classify, validate, authenticate, or assess synthetic media, nor provide determinations regarding originality, authorship, or authenticity.
