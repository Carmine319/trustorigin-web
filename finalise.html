Finalisation & Declaration

The Finalisation & Declaration vertical enables formal declaration and finalisation of artefacts within the TrustOrigin registry. It records explicit statements of completion, intent, or status without validating their truth or legal effect.

This vertical is suitable for academic, professional, and institutional contexts where the act of declaration itself must be recorded neutrally.

This system does not validate the truth, accuracy, or legal status of declared artefacts.
