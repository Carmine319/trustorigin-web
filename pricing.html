Enterprise Pricing & Licensing Structure

TrustOrigin operates under a structured tier model designed to support progressive institutional integration. Pricing reflects the depth of documentation articulation, governance consolidation, and continuity architecture required by the adopting organisation.

The infrastructure maintains structural neutrality across all tiers. Commercial engagement does not alter the non-claims boundary or introduce certification authority.

Tier I — Public Registry Access

This entry-level tier provides structured declarative recording capabilities for AI usage statements, digital artefact registration, and vendor dependency declarations. Each submission produces a Trust Receipt reflecting timestamp and cryptographic anchoring.

This tier is suitable for research teams, early-stage organisations, and institutions requiring durable documentation without governance consolidation.

Engagement Range: From £1,500

Tier II — Ethics Transparency Declaration Framework

This tier integrates structured Ethics Transparency articulation, including AI governance posture statements, human oversight models, vendor exposure mapping, and declared risk boundaries.

The output is suitable for board-level reporting preparation, procurement positioning, and regulatory dialogue contexts. Declarations are registry-anchored and structured for durable documentation.

Engagement Range: From £2,500

Tier III — Institutional Governance & Continuity Dossier

This tier consolidates registry declarations, ethics transparency articulation, vendor dependency mapping, and migration readiness posture into a structured institutional Dossier framework.

The Dossier provides governance narrative coherence suitable for regulated environments and enterprise-scale institutions requiring consolidated documentary clarity.

Engagement Range: £6,500 – £15,000

Tier IV — Enterprise Continuity Integration

This tier extends beyond documentation consolidation to incorporate organisation-wide governance layering, annual re-declaration protocols, version freeze architecture, and deterministic export bundle preparation.

Suitable for institutions with complex vendor exposure, multi-department AI deployment, or heightened regulatory scrutiny.

Engagement Range: £25,000+

Commercial Principles

  • Neutrality preserved across all tiers
  • No certification authority introduced at any pricing level
  • All declarations remain responsibility of declaring institution
  • Structured documentation scalability without governance overreach
  • Deterministic export capability for continuity preservation

Pricing may vary based on institutional complexity, number of departments integrated, vendor exposure depth, and required version governance protocols.

Non-Claims Boundary

TrustOrigin does not provide legal advice, compliance certification, regulatory validation, or ethical adjudication at any pricing tier. The infrastructure preserves declared posture in a time-anchored format without validating substantive correctness.

Responsibility for content accuracy remains solely with the declaring institution.
