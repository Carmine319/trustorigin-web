Artefact Registration

Organisations frequently operate with undocumented artefacts, undeclared AI usage, unrecorded vendor dependency, and unstructured migration posture.

Registration does not certify or validate. It records declared state at a specific point in time.

What Can Be Registered

  • AI system usage declarations
  • Policy documents
  • Contracts
  • Technical designs
  • Construction drawings
  • Music masters
  • Software builds
  • Vendor dependency records
  • Data continuity declarations

Structural Neutrality Notice

Registration records declared state only. TrustOrigin does not verify accuracy, authenticity, legality, ownership, compliance status, or regulatory sufficiency.

Interpretation remains solely with competent external authorities.
