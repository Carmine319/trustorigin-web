Ethical & Transparency Self-Declaration

This document outlines the declared operational posture of TrustOrigin as a neutral declarative governance infrastructure.

TrustOrigin records structured institutional statements. It does not certify, validate, audit, endorse, or adjudicate.

1. Operational Neutrality

  • No certification authority
  • No compliance validation
  • No legal advisory function
  • No regulatory endorsement
  • No dispute resolution role

TrustOrigin functions exclusively as a structured declarative recording layer.

2. Vendor Neutrality

  • No exclusive AI vendor alignment
  • No infrastructure lock-in strategy
  • No proprietary export constraints
  • No dependency enforcement mechanisms

Institutions retain full operational independence from TrustOrigin.

3. Transparency Commitments

  • Publication of governance boundaries
  • Publication of non-claims charter
  • Structured declaration schema visibility
  • Deterministic export availability
  • Versioned documentation discipline

TrustOrigin maintains documented boundaries between declarative recording and operational decision-making.

4. Data & Documentation Posture

  • No operational system control
  • No hidden data extraction mechanisms
  • No behavioural profiling
  • No undisclosed third-party data monetisation

Registry entries reflect declarant submissions at time of record creation. TrustOrigin does not independently verify factual accuracy.

5. Governance Evolution

This declaration is versioned and subject to structured revision. Future amendments will be documented and dated.

Boundary Reminder

TrustOrigin records structured statements of institutional posture. It does not determine legality, compliance status, technical safety, or regulatory sufficiency.

TrustOrigin is a neutral declarative governance infrastructure.
